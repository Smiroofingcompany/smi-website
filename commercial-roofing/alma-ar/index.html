Commercial Roofing Systems for Alma's Industrial and Commercial Properties

Alma's commercial mix spans large-footprint food processing, light industrial, retail, and institutional buildings. SMI installs the full spectrum of commercial roofing systems and recommends based on your building type, roof slope, and budget.

  • Metal Panel Roofing: The dominant choice for Alma's food processing and industrial facilities. Both standing seam and exposed-fastener metal panels deliver the durability and ease of maintenance that large commercial operations require. 40+ year lifespans with minimal ongoing cost.
  • TPO Roofing: The most energy-efficient flat-roof system for Alma's retail and commercial buildings. Reflective white membrane, long-term NDL manufacturer warranties, and proven performance in the Arkansas summer heat and storm seasons.
  • EPDM Roofing: Versatile rubber membrane well-suited to Crawford County's climate. Excellent performance on institutional and educational buildings, with outstanding UV and thermal resistance.
  • Standing Seam Metal: Premium choice for high-profile commercial buildings, schools, and retail centers in Alma wanting maximum aesthetics alongside 40+ year durability. Popular for new construction along the I-40 corridor.
  • Roof Coatings & Restoration: For existing Alma commercial roofs with serviceable structure, silicone or acrylic coatings extend service life by 10-15 years at significantly lower cost than full replacement.
