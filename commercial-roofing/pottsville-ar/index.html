Commercial Roofing Systems for Pottsville's Growing Corridor

Pottsville's commercial mix spans new retail construction, school facilities, agricultural commercial properties, and existing commercial buildings along US-64. SMI installs the right system for each building type rather than defaulting to a one-size-fits-all approach.

  • TPO Roofing: The most commonly specified system for new flat-roof commercial construction in Pottsville's US-64 corridor. Energy-efficient, widely warrantied, and proven in Arkansas's climate. NDL manufacturer warranties up to 20 years available.
  • Standing Seam Metal: The premium choice for Pottsville retail buildings, school facilities, and commercial construction wanting maximum curb appeal alongside 40+ year durability. Excellent wind and hail resistance for the River Valley storm season.
  • EPDM Roofing: Durable rubber membrane for institutional and commercial buildings with complex geometry or heavy rooftop equipment. Outstanding long-term performance in Pope County's climate.
  • Metal Panel Systems: Exposed-fastener metal panels deliver wide-span coverage at lower cost per square foot — the preferred choice for agricultural commercial buildings and larger warehouse-style properties in eastern Pope County.
  • Roof Coatings & Restoration: For existing Pottsville commercial roofs with serviceable structure, silicone or acrylic coatings provide 10-15 additional years of watertight performance at a fraction of replacement cost.
