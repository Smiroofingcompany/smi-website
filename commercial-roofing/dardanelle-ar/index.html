Is It Time for Commercial Roofing in Dardanelle?

Dardanelle's commercial mix includes civic and institutional buildings, agricultural processing, retail, and river-adjacent recreational structures. Each category has distinct roofing requirements and SMI handles all of them.

  • Standing Seam Metal: The most popular choice for Yell County agricultural and rural commercial properties. Excellent wind resistance, minimal maintenance, and a 40+ year lifespan make it the right long-term investment for properties outside dense commercial zones.
  • TPO Roofing: The go-to flat-roof system for Dardanelle's retail and commercial buildings. Energy-efficient white membrane, NDL manufacturer warranties up to 20 years, and proven performance in Arkansas's climate extremes.
  • EPDM Roofing: Durable rubber membrane widely used on institutional buildings and properties with complex roof geometry. Outstanding UV and thermal resistance for the River Valley's heat and sun exposure.
  • Metal Panel Systems: For large-footprint agricultural processing facilities and warehouses in Yell County, exposed-fastener metal panel systems deliver maximum coverage at a lower cost per square foot than standing seam.
  • Roof Coatings & Restoration: Silicone and acrylic coatings extend the life of existing commercial roofs by 10-15 years. An excellent option for Dardanelle commercial building owners who want to defer replacement while eliminating leaks and improving energy efficiency.
