What changes price

The estimate should explain the roof, not just the total.

SMI keeps pricing tied to visible conditions and photos. That means you can see why the scope includes a repair, replacement, coating, claim item, or upgrade before approving work.

Replacement is not the only option

If the roof is dry and structurally sound, coatings or repair may extend service life for less than replacement. SMI verifies eligibility before recommending restoration.

Insulation changes everything

Commercial estimates can change when insulation, R-value, trapped moisture, deck repair, edge metal, drainage, or code requirements enter the scope.

Facility downtime matters

Access, tenant operations, safety planning, weather windows, material staging, and cleanup all affect the schedule and the real cost of commercial work.
