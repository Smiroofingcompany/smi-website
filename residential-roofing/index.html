Signs Your Roof May Need Replacement

  • Your roof is 18-25 years old or near the end of its service life.
  • Shingles are missing, curling, cracked, brittle, or losing granules.
  • Granule loss leaves shingles bald, patchy, or thin in high-wear areas.
  • Repeated leaks, ceiling stains, or soft decking show up after rain.
  • Sagging roof planes, wavy decking, or visible dips appear from the ground.
  • Storm damage is spread across several roof slopes instead of one small area.
  • You want a written repair-versus-replacement scope before deciding.

Shingle Systems We Install

The right shingle system depends on your budget, your home's architecture, and how long you plan to stay. We install every major product line and help you choose the system that makes the most sense for your situation.

Architectural shingles are the most popular choice for Arkansas homes. They deliver a dimensional, layered look with 30-year to lifetime warranties. GAF Timberline HDZ, Owens Corning Duration, and CertainTeed Landmark are the three systems we install most frequently.

Premium designer shingles replicate the look of natural slate, cedar shake, or old-world tile at a fraction of the cost and weight. These are ideal for custom homes, historic neighborhoods, and homeowners who want maximum curb appeal.

3-tab shingles are a budget-friendly option for rental properties, outbuildings, and situations where cost efficiency is the priority. They carry 20-25 year warranties and provide reliable weather protection.

Our Roof Replacement Process

Every SMI roof replacement follows the same proven process, whether it's a 1,200-square-foot starter home or a 5,000-square-foot estate.

Free inspection and estimate: We inspect your roof, document everything with photos, and provide a detailed written estimate. If insurance is involved, we handle the adjuster meeting and paperwork.

Material selection: You choose your shingle system, color, and accessories. We bring samples to your home so you can see how they look against your siding, brick, and trim.

Tear-off and deck inspection: We remove the old roofing system down to the deck. If we find rotted decking, damaged trusses, or other structural issues, we repair them before installing new materials. No surprises, no hidden charges.

Installation: New underlayment, ice and water shield in valleys and at eaves, drip edge, starter strip, field shingles, ridge ventilation, and ridge cap. Every component installed to manufacturer specifications so your warranty is fully valid.

Final walkthrough and cleanup: Magnetic sweeps of your entire yard. Debris hauled off. Final photos documenting the completed project. We walk you through the finished roof and answer every question.

  • Inspection: We inspect roof surfaces, attic clues, drainage, penetrations, and system details before recommending work.
  • Written estimate: You get photos, measurements, scope options, and pricing before scheduling.
  • Scheduling: We set timing around weather, material availability, access, and urgency.
  • Installation or repair: The crew completes the approved installation, repair, coating, inspection follow-up, or claim-supported work.
  • Cleanup: We protect the property, collect debris, run magnet sweeps when nails are present, and document the finished roof.
  • Warranty review: We review workmanship warranty, material coverage, maintenance notes, and next steps before closing the job.
