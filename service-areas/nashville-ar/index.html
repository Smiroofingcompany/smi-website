Roofing Services for Nashville and Howard County

Nashville is the county seat of Howard County in southwest Arkansas, surrounded by the rolling hills and timber country where the Ouachita Mountains begin to flatten toward the Gulf Coastal Plain. The community supports a tight-knit residential base along with small businesses, the Nashville School District, and healthcare facilities that all depend on reliable roofing.

SMI Roofing serves Nashville and all of Howard County with residential roof replacement, storm damage repair, insurance claim handling, and metal roofing installations for residential, agricultural, and commercial properties. Southwest Arkansas is well within our service area, and we treat every Howard County customer with the same commitment that has earned us 231+ five-star Google reviews.

Residential Roofing in Nashville, AR

Homes in Nashville range from post-war craftsman bungalows near the downtown square to newer ranch-style construction in subdivisions east of US-27. Many Nashville homes have roofs installed in the 1990s and early 2000s that are approaching the end of their functional lifespan. When you combine age with southwest Arkansas's severe thunderstorm season — peak hail activity runs April through June — you have a market where a significant percentage of homes have undetected storm damage.

We install GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed architectural shingle systems with 50-year material warranties and our own 10-year workmanship guarantee. For Nashville homeowners who want superior hail resistance and a 50+ year lifespan, we also install standing seam and exposed fastener metal roofing systems that are especially popular for both residential and agricultural buildings throughout the Ouachita region.

Storm Damage and Insurance Claims in Howard County

Southwest Arkansas sits in the tornado and severe thunderstorm corridor that runs from Oklahoma through the region seasonally. Howard County experiences hail-producing storms multiple times per year during spring storm season, and the resulting granule loss and impact damage often goes undetected for years. SMI's free post-storm inspection identifies damage that adjusters would have found — and our teams document everything needed to support a successful insurance claim.

We handle the entire insurance process for Nashville homeowners: free inspection, claim filing, adjuster meeting on your roof, supplement filing when initial estimates fall short, and denial appeals when warranted. Most Nashville customers pay only their deductible. Learn more about storm damage repair.
